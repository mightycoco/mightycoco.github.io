Active two channel, 6 way multiple expandable in master/slave configuration via pack-panel connection.

GRANDAU6 is an active multipe with two channels in 4HE which can work stand alone or connected together via a 4Pin Dupont/JST connector on the back.

When connected on the back you can route an input signal on channel A or B to the second module on the other end of your case. I.e. plug clock and reset from your sequencer in channels A & B and multiply to your desired modules. The second GRANDAU6 at the other end of your rack will receive the same clock and reset signals and multiply them further to other modules.

Multiple use-cases require setting jumpers on the back.
