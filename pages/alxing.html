ALXING is a 10hp motif generator creating a sequence of quantized CV values. The output can be clamped to a specific scale and the density of available notes can be reduced via the density parameter.

The term for it is "Constrained Random CV Generator", but one can think of it as a Turing Machine on steroids. It's designed to have good performance capabilities and also to bring additional functions with it, easing the usage.

The quantiser for example makes it a lot more convenient to work with it melodically and the plethora of scales let it spit out fresh tunes every time. Also a clock with selectable subdivisions or different playmodes are build in to spice up the musical figures just discovered, only to name a few.

Secondly the preset save and recall option comes in handy if one likes to go back to known places within the composition or use it to build a song like structure using saved sequences.

Talking about Modulation. Alxing implements cv-able main parameters and a freely routable MOD in- or output to interact with it's compounds even more deeply. Don't miss out on this package of endless fun, steering through happy accidents and a landscape of motifs to reveal.

Main Controls
(F!)
Pressing and holding the (F!) button and turning a potentiometer changes the alternative parameter of a knob.
Short Pressing the (F!) button opens the main menu.
variation After reaching the end of the sequence defined by length, one of the following variations is applied
shift
The sequence will be rolled to the right and a new random value will be fetched for the first step
stp 100%
All of the steps will receive a new random value
stp 75%
3/4th of the steps will receive a new random value
stp 50%
half of the steps will receive a new random value
stp 25%
1/4th of the steps will receive a new random value
loop
The sequence isn't changed and will loop forever
seq 25%
All steps get a new random value. Intensity of randomness is 0-25%
seq 50%
All steps get a new random value. Intensity of randomness is 0-50%
seq 75%
All steps get a new random value. Intensity of randomness is 0-75%
seq 100%
All steps get a new random value. Intensity of randomness is 0-100%
